SHIMLA (QUEEN OF HILLS)

A wonderful abode in the Himalayas, the city of Shimla is surrounded by scenic pine-covered hills and snow-capped peaks. Located in the magnificent hill state of Himachal Pradesh in India, it is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the country. From honeymooners to adventure seekers, the city serves as a charming retreat to a large number of people. The enchanting beauty of this hill station has attracted countless filmmakers and cinematographers. This erstwhile summer capital of the British, still retains its colonial-era charm through its spectacular historical churches and institutes, like Christ Church, Indian Institute of Advanced Study and many more.

Short Tentative Itinerary

1st Day: Shimla Arrival | Local Sightseeing
2nd Day: Full day trip to Kufri - Jakhoo hills
3rd Day: Departure

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1: SHIMLA ARRIVAL | LOCAL SIGHTSEEING


Leave Delhi at 6 AM in the morning, from Pre decided Destination. Reach Shimla by Afternoon. Check-in at the hotel and freshen up. Get ready to Visit Christ Church, Jakhoo Temple, Kalibari Temple, Lakkar Bazaar and Local Market. Back at the hotel by night. Dinner and Overnight stay at Shimla.


DAY 2 : FULL DAY TRIP TO KUFRI - JAKHOO HILLS


Wake-up in the morning and get mesmerized by the breathtaking sunrise. Post Break-fast visit Kufri, Green Valley, Mini Zoo, Chini Bungalow, White Flower Hall, Bhimakali Temple, Indira Gandhi Tourist Park . Evening free to stroll in the famous shopping place of Shimla town. Dinner and overnight at Shimla.


DAY 3 :  DEPARTURE


Wake-up in the morning and have breakfast. Check out from the hotel and enjoy time for shopping. In the afternoon depart from Delhi. Reach back to Delhi by night. End the trip with beautiful memories
