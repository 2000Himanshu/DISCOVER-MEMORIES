Mcleodganj & Triund

Mcleodganj is a hill station near Dharamshala, popular among trekkers. Located in Kangra district, Mcleodganj's culture is a beautiful blend of Tibetan with some British influence.Also known as Little Lhasa and famous around the world for being home to the Tibetan spiritual leader Dalai Lama, Mcleod Ganj is a beautiful town situated near upper Dharamsala. Nestled amidst majestic hills and lush greenery, this town is culturally blessed by a prominent Tibetan influence owing to the major settlement of Tibetans here. Mcleodganj has one of the most mesmerising landscapes in the entire state of Himachal Pradesh and attracts a lot of tourists throughout the year. The towns of Dharamsala, Mcleodganj, Bhagsu Nag and Kangra are situated very close to each other and tourists must cover all these destinations while travelling here.

Short Tentative Itinerary

0th Day: Start From New Delhi
1st Day: Arrive Mcleodganj, Visit Monastery and Waterfalls
2nd Day: Trek to Triund .Night stay near Triund
3rd Day: Return to McleodGanj and Departure for Delhi

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1: Arrive Mcleodganj, Visit Monastery and Waterfalls


Leave Delhi at 7PM, Pick up from Kashmiri Gate/Majnu ka Tila (Day 0). Reach Mcloedganj by morning, Check-in to hotel and freshen up. Post refreshment, explore the streets of Mcleodganj, visit Bhagsunag Waterfall and Bhagsunag Temple. Visit the Dalai Lama Monastery and cafes of Mcleodganj. Dinner and Overnight stay at Mcloedganj.


DAY 2 : Triund trek


Wake up early in the morning and after breakfast check out from hotel, begin your scenic trek to Triund. The trail takes you through the dense forests of pine with some scenic views of valleys. After 4-5 hours of trek, you will reach the Triund Top. Enjoy the beauty of the Himalayas and experience the mesmerising sunset from the top. Reach the campsite and dinner and overnight stay in the camps near Triund.


DAY 3 :  Trek down | Departure


Wake up and get mesmerized by the breathtaking views of Dhauladhars. Post breakfast, start your trek down to Mcleodganj from Triund. Reach Mcleodganj by afternoon. Explore cafe and market if time allows. In the evening, depart back to Delhi. Reach Delhi the next morning with Beautiful memories.
