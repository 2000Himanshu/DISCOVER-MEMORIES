Parashar Lake

Amongst the scenic and spiritual valleys of Himachal Pradesh lies the mystical Prashar Lake. Located near Mandi, the lake is a sacred destination nestled between soaring, snowbound mountains of the Dhauladhar Ranges of Kullu. The three storied temple stands tall on the banks. This thirteenth-century old pagoda is in honour of the Rishi, Prashar. The lake holds mythological importance that dates back to the era of Pandavas. The mystery of a floating island on the lake, the mesmerizing vistas, and the serene tranquillity echoes in the surroundings of the lake. Prashar is a popular destination and a must-visit location for travellers who feel bliss enveloped in mother nature. Tourists looking for adventure can engage in camping and trekking in and around the vicinity. The site is a marvellous natural phenomenon that will leave you perplexed yet mystified. The panoramic views of the valley and the landscape encircling the lake of Prashar is enthanting to tourists and the locals. You can visit the lake at any time of the year.

Short Tentative Itinerary

0 Day: Delhi to Mandi
1st Day: Mandi to Baggi; Parashar Lake Trek
2nd Day: Downhill Trek From Parashar Lake to Baggi

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1: Baggi to Prashar Lake


Leave Delhi at 8:00 pm in the night (Day 0). Reach Baggi village in the morning, which is the starting point of the Prashar Lake trek. It takes around 4-5 hours to reach the Prashar lake. Prashar lake is pristine and is surrounded by green pastures. There is a pagoda temple standing near the lake. After reaching there, explore the Prashar lake and pay homage at Prashar Rishi Temple. Have dinner and bonfire in the night. Overnight stay in tents.


DAY 2 : Prashar Lake to Baggi to Delhi


Wake-up early in the morning and enjoy the beautiful sunrise. After breakfast, start downhill trek towards Baggi. It takes around 3 hours to reach Baggi village. On reaching Baggi, depart back to Delhi. Reach Delhi the next morning with Beautiful memories.
