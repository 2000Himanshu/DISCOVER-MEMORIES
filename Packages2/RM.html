Mussoorie & Rishikesh

Mussoorie is famous as “The Queen of Hills”, Rishikesh is famous as “The Holy Abode of God” and our tour package is famous for offering you the delights of both Mussoorie and Rishikesh. On one hand we allow you to enjoy the sight of the gushing Kempty falls, hill altitude Lal Tibba and well maintained Botanical Gardens of Mussoorie and on the other hand we make your soul rejuvenate by a visit to the pious ancient shrines of Rishikesh like Swargashram, Mansa and Chandi Devi Temple. Thus, you get the pleasure of two wonderful destinations in one tour package.

Short Tentative Itinerary

1st Day: Rishikesh Arrival
2nd Day: Rafting and Local Sightseeing
3rd Day: Mussoorie arrival and sightseeing
4th Day: Sightseeing | Departure

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1: Rishikesh Arrival


Leave Delhi early in the morning. Reach Rishikesh by afternoon, Check-in to the hotel and freshen up. Evening free for leisure, enjoy famous Aarati of Holy Ganges at parmarth Niketan. Overnight stay and dinner at a hotel in Rishikesh.


DAY 2 : Rafting and Local Sightseeing


Wake-up early in the morning and enjoy the beautiful sunrise. After breakfast get ready to explore the beautiful rishikesh. Drive to rafting point. White water river rafting the most famous adventure activity of Rishikesh. Later explore the local market of Rishikesh, Ram Jhula, Lakshman Jhula, Beatles Ashram and more famous spots. Overnight stay and dinner at a hotel in Rishikesh.


DAY 3 :  Mussoorie arrival and sightseeing


Wake up in the morning and after breakfast drive to Mussoorie. On arrival, Check-in to the hotel and freshen up and get ready to explore Mussoorie. Visit the famous Kempty waterfalls (It is a popular tourist point in Mussoorie). Have lunch and visit Camels Back Road (The place offers a picturesque view of the sunset and a life-like resemblance of a sitting camel). Later visit Gun Hill Point (second highest point in Mussoorie). In the evening visit Gun hill road and mall road for shopping. Overnight stay and dinner at a hotel in Mussoorie.


DAY 4 :  Sightseeing & Departure


Wake-up early in the morning and enjoy the beautiful sunrise. After breakfast explore the remaining sightseeings of Mussoorie. Visit the famous Mussoorie Lake, Mall Road, Mussoorie and Lake Mist. After Lunch, depart back to Delhi. Reach Delhi by evening with Beautiful memories.
