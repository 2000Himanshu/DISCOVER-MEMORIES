MANALI ATAL TUNNEL

Manali is a charming town situated in the Kullu valley. Manali, once served as the ancient trade route. Today it is an abode for modern creativity with astounding hills and adventurous activities, and the town is a true heaven for culture lovers, foodies and peace seekers. The high altitude allows you to experience the perfect Himalayan ambience and lets you cruise your way through famous Rohtang Pass.The Himalayas have inspired many foreign settlements here, giving rise to popular European and Israeli cafes, restaurants and hostels, providing one a consortium of around the world cultures. Manali also has an array of breathtaking treks and sights for its nature lovers, for instance, the Patalsu Peak, Jogini Falls and the Rahala Falls. Restaurants and cafes attract foodies for their culinary justice to everything from Thai to European.

Short Tentative Itinerary

0 Day: Delhi to Manali
1st Day: Manali Arrival | Local Sightseeing
2nd Day: Solang Valley; Atal Tunnel
3rd Day: Departure

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1: REACH MANALI | LOCAL SIGHTSEEING


Leave Delhi at 7PM, Pick up from Kashmiri Gate/Majnu ka Tila (Day 0). Reach Manali by morning. Check-in at the hotel and freshen up. Get ready to explore Local Manali- Hidimba Temple, Vashisht Village, etc. Back at the hotel by night. Dinner and Overnight stay at Manali.


DAY 2 : SOLANG VALLEY | ATAL TUNNEL


Wake up early, had breakfast and ﻿Get ready for a full day excursion at Solang Valley. Solang Valley is a beautiful place and famous for adventure activities like Skiing, Snow Motor Ride, Ropeway, Etc. Later Drive towards Keylong, cross the Atal Tunnel and enjoy the mesmerising view of Lahaul Valley. Back to hotel by evening & Dinner and Overnight stay in Manali.


DAY 3 :  DEPARTURE


In the morning, have a delicious breakfast and prepare to check-out from the hotel. Day at Leisure for shopping. By afternoon Depart for Delhi. Reach Delhi by night, End the trip with beautiful memories.
