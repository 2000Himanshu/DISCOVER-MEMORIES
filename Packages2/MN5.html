SPITI VALLEY (5N/6D)

Spiti basically means 'middle land,' and it is called so because it is situated in the land between India and Tibet. This majestic cold desert valley is jam-packed with Beautiful Monasteries, lush green pine forests, green meadows and ruged villages that will leave you awe-struck. Spiti is a rustic and heavenly beautiful travel destination. It is a paradise for adventure seekers and admirers of Buddhism and phenomenal mountain vistas.

Short Tentative Itinerary

0 Day: Delhi - Manali
1st Day: Manali Arrival
2nd Day: Manali to Kaza ( 203 kms, 9 hrs)
3rd Day: Dhankar to Kaza via Kee and Kibber (90 kms, 4-5 hrs of drive)
4th Day: Kaza to Dhankar via Komic and Langza ( 120 kms, 6-7 hrs of drive)
5th Day: Kaza to Chandrataal (90 kms, 7 hrs of drive)
6th Day: Chandratal to Manali.(125 kms, 7 hrs drive)
7th Day: Reach Delhi

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 0 : DELHI TO MANALI


Depart from Delhi via Volvo in the evening. Overnight journey to Manali.


DAY 1 : MANALI


Reach Manali by morning and check-in to the hotel/swiss camps. After some leisure, feel free to explore the local area of Manali according to the place that interests you. You can visit the Mall road, Hidimba Devi Temple, Old Manali for some good cafes to chill or Vashisht temple for the Hot water Spring. Come back to the hotel/camps by evening. Dinner will be served and overnight stay at the hotel.


DAY 2 : MANALI – ROHTANG PASS – KUNZUM LA – KAZA


Today you will get a vehicle which will take you to Spiti Valley. Post early breakfast, we start our journey to the valley of the Gods – Lahaul & Spiti! Crossover to Lahaul by passing through the adventure haven – The Rohtang Pass, and watch the terrain shift from lush green valleys to brown arid deserts. At 14,931 ft we cross another high altitude pass & the gateway to the Spiti Valley – Kunzum La. We continue our drive through the stunning landscape to reach Kaza by evening. Hotel check in. Dinner will be served and overnight stay at Kaza.


DAY 3 :  KAZA - KEY MONASTERY - KIBBER - PIN VALLEY - KAZA


Wake up early and have your breakfast. Leave for Key Monastery. Being remotely located atop a hill overlooking endless plains, the Key Monastery is a world famous center for learning and is an obvious choice for those seeking peace and calm. Declared a national park in 1987, Pin Valley has altitudes ranging from 11,000 to 20,000 feet and is home to at least 12 endangered snow leopards. In addition, other animals indigenous to the region and park are the Siberian Ibex, Bharal, Weasel, Red Fox, and Marten. Birds such as the Pika, Griffon, Golden Eagle, Bearded Vulture, Chukor, and Raven can be spotted flocking to the skies of the picturesque valley. We first visit the Kungri Monastery and then the beautiful hamlet of Mud – the last motorable village of the Pin Valley. Dinner will be served and overnight stay at Kaza.


DAY 4 :  KAZA - HIKKIM - KOMIC - LANGZA - KAZA


Wake up early and have your breakfast. Visit some of the highest inhabited regions of the world. The highest village in Asia – Komic, situated at a height of 4513 m is cut off from rest of the world for most parts of the year. Later, we drive to Hikkim, where we have a chance to send postcards from the world’s highest post office! At Langza, walk back to the prehistoric era when Spiti was submerged by the Tethys Sea, as you explore a land very rich with fossils of Marine animals and plants. Dinner will be served and overnight stay at Kaza.


DAY 5 :   KAZA - CHICHAM - LOSAR - KUNZUM LA - CHANDRATAAL LAKE


Wake up early and have your breakfast. We start tracing our steps back towards Manali. However, make one last stop to witness another Spitian wonder – Chandrataal Lake. The mystical and beautiful Chandrataal Lake or the Moon Lake is located at a height of 14,000 feet. The : Chandrabhaga mountain range forms a striking backdrop for the lake, which changes appearance according to the pictures painted in the sky. Later check in to the campsite near Chandratal Lake. Dinner will be served and overnight stay at the tents under the gaze of a million stars and the Milky Way Galaxy.


DAY 6 :  CHANDRATAAL LAKE - MANALI


Wake up early and have your breakfast. We will leave towards Manali. Reach manali in evening. Transfer to volvo bus stand and board your bus for Delhi. Overnight journey to Delhi.


DAY 7 :  Delhi


Reach Delhi early in the morning with lots of amazing memories to remember for a lifetime!
